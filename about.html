About Me

I'm a Haitian-Salvadoran concept artist and illustrator specializing in character design, visual development, and sequential art. I've worked on character concepts and visual development for mobile games and webtoon projects, creating everything from character design and expression sheets to environment design and narrative consultation. I'm an avid reader and gamer and fairly decent roller-blader.

What I Do

  • Character concept art and design sheets
  • Comic/webtoon illustration and page layouts
  • Visual development for games and interactive media
  • Lore and narrative consultation
  • Environment and prop design
